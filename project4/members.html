The Aggressor Force Team

Members Only, and we don’t just mean the mid-80’s jacket maker! Titus Industries started off with just a few friends and has grown to not just be the innovators of dynamic and modular airsoft fields, but with formed our own team, The Aggressor Force Team!

We welcome new players into our team; however, you must meet a strict set of rules and expectations. Can you handle it? Contact Us us or stop by the Titus Industries | Aggressor Force team field for more details.

Our Members

Our members are awesome! We love this sport and we protect it to the best of our ability. If you want to be an Aggressor Force Team member or you just want a basic account on Titus Industries.com, just contact us and fill out the request form.


Members get the privilege to post pictures on our site and they get exclusive offers and discounts at Titus Industries and the Aggressor Force game field and shop.
