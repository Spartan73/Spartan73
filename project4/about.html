About Us

Our Adventure

We are Airsofters and we have a unique passion for the sport! Titus Industries and our airsoft team, Aggressor Force, created a unique and modular airsoft field designed specifically with die-hard players in mind.


This dream started about 6 years ago among close friends. We thought to ourselves, how can we change field dynamics and bring new challenges to our sport? How can we make our field stand out from the rest and lead the pack? How can we ensure our field is always dynamically changing and challenging; something our competition ends up losing to in the end… field repetitiveness can be fatal when talented players are so good at memorizing course layouts, they obliterate the competition and this takes the fun out of it for new and old players.


Our solution? Why not build field barricades, walls and other structures that can easily be moved around and put together with relative ease? How about we make a field using the Lego building blocks format? Something that can be modified for chartered games or changed quarterly so that our patrons can experience something new!


Well, our dream materialized over years of planning and multiple collaboration efforts and now our field is unique in that it is modular in design like Lego building blocks and the dynamics of the field can change at any time or upon request for chartered game days or to meet team desires.


We’re glad you found our website and we hope you spend some time browsing around. If you’re in the Washington State area, we invite you to come check out our shop and field and if you’re a field owner, or starting one and you want something unique, we also fabricate and sell modular pieces.



Thank you, from all of us at Titus Industries and the Aggressor Force Team...Enjoy your visit!
