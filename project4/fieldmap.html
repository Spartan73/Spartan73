Game Field Layout

The Titus Industries and Aggressor Force Team field is modular and dynamic. The field barricades, walls and other related obstacles and structures, could be changed at any time in order to provide a unique playing experience thus making the dynamics hard to memorize or get used to. This unique dynamic field design also makes game play challenging for those that strive to improve their skills rather than memorizing the field by muscle-memory.


Titus Industries and the Aggressor Force Team intend to do their part for the earth and our environment. Our modular field parts are made from 80% recycled materials and we’re hoping to increase that to well over 95% as we source alternate building methods and materials. The beauty of these modular parts is that it is very much like LEGOS and everyone knows those world-famous building blocks! There’s nothing quite like building huge life-size LEGO blocks, trust us, we know!


The Dynamic and Modular Build
